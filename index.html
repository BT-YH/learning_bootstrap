About me:


I am an avid learner in the fields of physics and computer science. I have research experience in deep learning and computer vision, and I am equipped with physics lab techniques. On the other hand, I love teaching people about physics and computer science.

I have worked as a teaching assistant for physics, CS, and data science classes. I am interested in the intersection of the two fields, ie. applying machine learning models to analyze experimental data, quantum AI and ML, and developing software tools for physics simulation.

As you might have guessed, I am a physics and CS geek, a problem solver, and a huge collaborator.

If you are interested in working together, please contact me!
